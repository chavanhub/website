About

Dr. Umesh B. Chavan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Technology at Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli — one of Maharashtra's premier engineering institutions. He brings a rare combination of over six years of industry experience at Alfa Laval (India) Ltd. and more than 25 years in academia.

His doctoral research, completed in 2022 from Shivaji University, Kolhapur, focused on deep convolutional neural networks and parallel computing for facial expression recognition. He has been a pioneer in bringing High Performance Computing (HPC) and parallelism into undergraduate curricula, presenting at international venues including New Orleans, USA.

Beyond teaching, Dr. Chavan serves on the Board of Studies in Computer Engineering at Shivaji University and as Convener of the Central Library, demonstrating sustained commitment to institution-building and academic governance.
